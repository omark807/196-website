Illinois' premier freshman honors CS class, by students, for students.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower students to create projects from square one, familiarizing themselves with the software development cycle, learning how to work effectively in teams, and get a chacne to learn technologies that extend beyond the introductory course. By the end of the class, students willl have gained the following skills crucial to becoming talented computer scientists:

Workflow

Understanding the software workflow can be a daunting task at first, but don't worry! We'll guide you every step of the way.

Teamwork

One of the most essential qualities of successful programmers is the ability to work effectibely in teams. In CS 196, you'll get the chance to experience the benefits and challenges of working on a project with a team, figuring out one another's strengths and weakenesses to create a project that incorporates pieces of everyone's ideas.

An Idea

Have an idea for a project that you've always wanted to work on? Get a chance to do so!

Knowledge

Expand your skill set beyond that gained in CS 125 through exposure to programming languages quite different than Java, as well as any technologies you learn throuhgout the development of your project.
